# Nimbus Billing FAQ

Frequently asked questions about Nimbus pricing. Nimbus is fictional sample data
for the RAG evaluation. This document is a distractor: it shares vocabulary with
the other docs but does not answer the CLI or deployment questions.

## Plans

Nimbus offers three plans:

- **Free** — one project, `us-east` only, community support.
- **Team** — unlimited projects, all regions, email support, $49/month.
- **Enterprise** — custom pricing, SSO, dedicated support, and an SLA.

## Billing cycle

Paid plans are billed monthly in advance. You can switch plans at any time; the
change is prorated on your next invoice.

## Refunds

Monthly plans are non-refundable, but you can cancel before the next cycle to
avoid further charges. Enterprise contracts follow their own terms.
